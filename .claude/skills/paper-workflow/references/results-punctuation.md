# Results Em-Dash Convention

Results sections use em-dashes primarily for result/conclusion statements and contrast. The em-dash often replaces "therefore" or "whereas".

## Chinese patterns

| —— function | Fix | Before | After |
|-------------|-----|--------|-------|
| **Result** — B follows from A | `，因此` / `，这意味着` | `需调整K个模式——至少需秩K` | `需调整K个模式，因此至少需秩K` |
| **Contrast** — B contrasts A | `，而` / `，但` | `1D缩小或反转——2D中系统性超越` | `1D缩小或反转，而2D中系统性超越` |
| **Elaboration** — B specifies A | `。具体而言` | `LoRA方差更低——20种子高度集中` | `LoRA方差更低；20种子高度集中` |

## English patterns

| — function | Fix | Before | After |
|------------|-----|--------|-------|
| **Result** | "therefore" / "meaning" / appositive comma | "error drops to 0.065 — a 45% reduction" | "error drops to 0.065, a 45% reduction" |
| **Contrast** | "whereas" / "while" | "1D shrinks — 2D systematically outperforms" | "1D shrinks, whereas 2D systematically outperforms" |
| **Parenthetical** | commas | "full FT — with 100% parameters — is unstable" | "full FT, with 100% parameters, is unstable" |

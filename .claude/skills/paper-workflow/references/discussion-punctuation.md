# Discussion Em-Dash Convention

Discussion sections use em-dashes for implication, conclusion, and offering alternative explanations. Two common patterns: (1) result chains where B follows from A, and (2) alternative hypotheses separated by a dash.

## Chinese patterns

| —— function | Fix | Before | After |
|-------------|-----|--------|-------|
| **Conclusion** | `，因此` / `，说明` | `有效秩9-48——低秩结构主导` | `有效秩9-48，说明低秩结构主导` |
| **Alternative** | `；或` / `；` | `取最简解释——替代需r=64` | `取最简解释；替代需r=64` |
| **Elaboration** | `。具体而言` | `存在局限——统计功效不足` | `存在统计局限：N=20下部分对比...` |

## English patterns

| — function | Fix | Before | After |
|------------|-----|--------|-------|
| **Implication** | "suggesting that" / "indicating" | "9–48 modes carry signal — the rest is noise" | "9–48 modes carry signal, indicating the rest is noise" |
| **Alternative** | "; alternatively," / "; or" | "rank 16 is optimal — rank 32 shows no gain" | "rank 16 is optimal; rank 32 shows no gain" |
| **Concession** | "although" / "while" | "LoRA wins — its AD cost is higher" | "LoRA wins, although its AD cost is higher" |

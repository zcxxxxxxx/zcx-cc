# Article Usage (a/an/the) — Key Rules

## Basic Rules
- Singular countable noun first mention: use "a/an"
- Singular countable noun subsequent mention: use "the"
- Plural/general concepts: no article
- Most abstract nouns and mass nouns: no article

## Common Errors by Chinese Writers
- ❌ "Equation can be solved by" → ✅ "The equation can be solved by"
- ❌ "The convergence is fast" (general statement) → ✅ "Convergence is fast"
- ❌ "We propose new method" → ✅ "We propose a new method"
- ❌ "The PINNs were introduced in 2019" (general concept) → ✅ "PINNs were introduced in 2019"

## Quick Check
- "a" = general, first introduction, any one of many
- "the" = specific, already introduced, unique in context
- no article = general concept, plural, uncountable
